What is the Lombardi Project?

Arguably, the 2 most important aspect of improving at Melee are execution and pattern recognition. Players who are prepared for a scenario and understand the better options and counterplay to those options are going to come out on top more often than the players who are experiencing that situation for the first time. Practicing exectution for basic tech is fairly straightforward these days when you have tools like UnclePunch's Training Lab. However, it becomes more difficult when the execution that needs training is a series of actions in a scenario that can be altered quickly by an opponent.

With the latest update to Uncle Punch's Training Mode, training yourself to recognize those scenarios and execute properly is easier now than ever before.

The Lombardi Project was built to house Training Mode savestate files, organize them by matchup and category, and offer search functionality. Instead of having to create a savestate yourself everytime you want to practice a scenario, you can search for it here!

The Lombardi Project is a work in progress and is constantly being updated. New scenarios are always being suggested and created. Feel free to join our Discord Server and message a site contributor with any suggestions!
