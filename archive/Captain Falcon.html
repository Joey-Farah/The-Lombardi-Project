Captain Falcon

Guess Man got ya down?
3 Rules for the Matchup
  1. High Lasers to stuff Nairs
  2. Remember Falcon's few OOS options
  3. Prepare to SDI Nair at low percents


The 3 rules in-depth:
1. Falcons are going to spam short hop Nair and Falco's high laser stuffs that. Be wary of how close you are when you start your lasers and if Falcon is starting to pick up on your high lasers. Falcon will look to side-b underneath your high lasers or dash dance grab.
2. Don't respect Falcon's shield. This doesn't me be careless on his shield, but understand that Falcon's OOS options are relatively weak. Be wary of stomp OOS by not Shining his shield too much and be ready to start reading rolls. Look for late aerial>shine grab>fthrow, late aerial waveshine back or late nair dash back to read roll in and maintain stage positioning.
3. Falcon's love to follow up their Nairs with grab, so be ready to SDI nair at low percent. It's reactable when you get hit as you laser sometimes when getting caught in other actions. Essentially, Falcon's aerials at low percent into a grab aren't always real, you have counterplay. See the scenario below.

Getting caught by Nair

Falcon's Nair will often catch you mid-laser and combo into a grab, but you can react mid-Nair to prevent Falcon from getting a follow-up.

Counterplay

On reaction to your SHL getting Naired, you can SDI and ASDI away for the second hit of Nair. This lets you dodge an immediate grab and whiff punish.
