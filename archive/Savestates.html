How to Use Savestate Files

  1. Ensure you have the latest version of Training Mode from Uncle Punch's Patreon.
  2. Click the "Savestate" button next to the "Counterplay" button on a matchup scenario page.
  3. Download and save the savestate file.
  4. Open your Dolphin emulator, click Emulation, Load State, and select the savestate file you just downloaded.
  5. Your 20XX ISO should open to the savestate recording. Just unpause and the recording will playback.

    6. DISCLAIMER: The 20XX savestates you'll find here won't replicate every variant you could experience in a scenario. A savestate will only capture one set of aerial drift, fast fall timing, act out of hitsun timing, etc. I encourage you to use these savestates as templates to do your own labbing and create your own 20XX recordings.
